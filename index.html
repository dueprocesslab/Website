Executive Summary
A 20-year retirement account was destroyed in 21 months through a documented cycle of solicitation, enablement, and concealment.
Jennifer Tautfest transferred $341,331 from a conservatively managed Fidelity IRA into a Robinhood Traditional IRA in 2023. Over the next 21 months, her account was subjected to Level 3 options trading approved in 12 days, 7,002 hidden execution fills on 316 orders, $44.9M in prohibited margin credit inside a protected retirement account, and platform gamification features that Massachusetts had ordered removed two years earlier. The account declined to $35,099 — a 95% market-adjusted loss while the S&P 500 gained 23%. This is the third time the same conduct pattern was applied to this investor across three separate Robinhood accounts.
